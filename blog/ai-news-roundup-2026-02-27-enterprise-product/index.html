What happened: Colorado's Artificial Intelligence Act (SB 24-205) ‚Äî one of the most comprehensive state AI laws in the U.S. ‚Äî is currently under active legislative review, with the Colorado General Assembly considering amendments during its 2026 session. 10. EU AI Act High-Risk AI Compliance Deadline: August 2, 2026 ‚Äî Five Months Out

What happened: Under the European Union AI Act, high-risk AI systems deployed in the financial sector ‚Äî including AI used for credit scoring, fraud detection, insurance risk assessment, and similar applications ‚Äî must meet full compliance requirements by August 2, 2026.
