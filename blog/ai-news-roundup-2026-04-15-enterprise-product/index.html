One Take

The AI industry is entering a stabilization phase where enterprise adoption and regulatory compliance are now equally important as raw capability improvements. We're seeing consolidation around a few major platforms (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google), while open-source alternatives (Mistral, Meta's Llama) carve out space for cost-conscious and privacy-focused organizations. The real shift is from "how do we build AI?" to "how do we deploy it responsibly at scale?" — and that's creating demand for governance tools, audit capabilities, and compliance frameworks. Action item: Audit your current LLM deployments against the tightening EU standards; non-compliance next quarter could trigger retroactive fines.
